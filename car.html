THE BMW X1

What Does It have to offer?

Let's Dive In

The BMW X1 is a line of cars produced by German marque BMW since 2009. It is in the subcompact luxury crossover class and the the first generation X1 was based on the E90 3 series and offered rear-wheel drive layout as standard. At it's introduction in 2009, it was positioned as the smallest SUV in the BMW line-up, below the X3 prior to the launch of the smaller X2. The X1 essentially aiming a wider range of customers due to it's smaller size, increased efficiency and a lower price tag due to the all-wheel drive layout.
